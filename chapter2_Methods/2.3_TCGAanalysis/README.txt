This directory contains the scripts, inputs and outputs corresponding to the procedure described in Section 2.3 TCGA analysis. The following steps are contained here:

- Obtaining TCGA metadata 
- Detecting tumour outliers 
- Estimation of tumor purity and impurity and recalibrating ESTIMATE

The steps of processing the raw sequencing data and fitting models of differential AS are included in Chapter2 Section 2.1_TRexMethod.
